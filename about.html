YE ZOU

Hi, thank you for visiting my site.

I obtain my Ph.D from Biochemistry and Molecular Biophysics department in Kansas State University. My major professor is Prof. Ho Leung Ng.
I am a scientist at Alkermes Inc. I am currently working on using computational methods to study the mechanisms of biological systems and using machine learning to study drug discovery. I have expertise in molecular dynamics simulations of protein in water and membrane protein in lipid bilayer and machine learning.
